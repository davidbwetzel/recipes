Chili Rojo (red chili) is basically beef
Ingredients:

	2 lbs ground beef
	1 head garlic
	2 lbs dried red chilis

Process:
	
Make enchilada sauce
	Boil the dried chilis in a large stock pot until tender (1-2 hours)
	remove chilis from stock pot and remove stems. reserve liquid
	blend the chilis (remove stems) with 5-6 cloves of garlic
	use some of the reserved chili liquid to thin out the sauce
